What is ELTE Library Services ?

The library network maintained by the Eötvös Loránd University (ELTE) has altogether about 3.6 million
documents. The University Library Service (ULS), the ELTE library network was formed in 2009 and consists
of more than 50 faculty, institute, department, dormitory, and school libraries.
